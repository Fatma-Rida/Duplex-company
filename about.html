Company Structure

Duplex Construction & Development is the main contracting and execution arm of our group, specializing in comprehensive construction solutions including structural works, concrete construction, and on-site execution.

Supporting this core activity, BUNYAN Engineering Consulting provides engineering design coordination, technical studies, value engineering, and continuous client support to ensure efficient planning and successful project delivery.

In addition, the Royal Concrete Factory, operating as a subsidiary of Duplex Construction & Development, supplies high-quality concrete products, ensuring consistency, quality control, and optimized timelines across all projects.

Together, our group operates as a fully integrated system, combining execution, consultancy, and material supply to deliver reliable and sustainable construction solutions.
