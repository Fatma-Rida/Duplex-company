Company Structure

Construction & Development is the main contracting and execution arm of our group, specializing in comprehensive construction solutions. The company is responsible for full project implementation, including structural works, concrete construction, and on-site execution, ensuring the delivery of high-quality projects in accordance with engineering standards and client requirements.

Supporting this core activity,BUNYAN Engineering Consulting plays a vital role as our specialized consultancy partner. The company provides engineering design coordination, technical studies, value engineering, and continuous client support, contributing to efficient planning and successful project delivery.

In addition, the Royal Concrete Factory , operating as a subsidiary of Duplex Construction & Development, supplies high-quality concrete products. This integration strengthens our ability to control quality, optimize timelines, and ensure consistency across all construction phases.

Together, our group operates as a fully integrated system, combining execution, consultancy, and material supply to deliver reliable, efficient, and sustainable construction solutions.
